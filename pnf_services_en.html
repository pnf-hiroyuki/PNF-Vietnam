PNF Application Examples

✔ Sports Athletes

  • Performance Enhancement → Improves muscle strength, flexibility, and explosive power
  • Injury Prevention → Balances joint stability and enhances proper muscle strength
  • Sports Rehabilitation → Supports recovery from sprains, muscle strains, and post-surgical conditions

✔ Rehabilitation (Neuromuscular & Motor Disorders)

  • Neurological Disorders → Effective for stroke, Parkinson’s disease, and spinal cord injuries
  • Chronic Pain & Post-Surgery Rehab → Relieves stiffness, back pain, and arthritis; aids recovery
  • Limited Range of Motion → Improve range of motion after surgery or for frozen shoulder

✔ General Population (Desk Workers & Seniors)

  • Desk Workers → Improves posture, prevents/relieves back pain & straight neck syndrome
  • Seniors → Enhances balance and reduces fall risks

◆ Follow-up Care After PNF Treatment

  • Monitoring Progress → Evaluates motion range, pain levels & adjusts programs as needed
  • Home Exercises → Covers stretching, movement correction, & daily workout tips
  • Preparation for the Next Session → Uses client feedback to refine treatment
