Traditional massage and stretching techniques can temporarily relax muscles and relieve pain. However, they only provide short-term relief and do not address the root cause of dysfunction.

PNF (Proprioceptive Neuromuscular Facilitation) is a rehabilitation technique designed to enhance neuromuscular coordination, optimize movement efficiency, and improve overall motor function.

ðŸ”¹ Why is Movement Essential?

Improving physical function requires more than just moving muscles. Proper coordination between the nervous system and muscles is crucial for efficient movement.

Therefore, instead of relying solely on stretching or massage, functional movement training is necessary.

PNF stimulates the brain and nervous system while activating the muscles, helping individuals develop correct movement patterns and enhance motor function.
